## Recommendations

To improve Botium Toys’ security posture and protect sensitive information, the following recommendations are suggested:

1. **Implement key controls**: Least Privilege, Separation of Duties, Disaster Recovery Plans, Password Policies, Intrusion Detection System (IDS), Encryption, and a centralized Password Management System.
2. **Regularly manage legacy systems**: Establish a schedule for monitoring, maintenance, and intervention procedures.
3. **Enhance compliance practices**: Properly classify and inventory all assets to identify gaps in PCI DSS, GDPR, and SOC standards.
4. **Maintain physical and technical safeguards**: Continue monitoring locks, CCTV, fire detection/prevention, firewall, and antivirus while addressing gaps in access control and IDS implementation.
5. **Ongoing staff awareness**: Ensure all employees understand policies and responsibilities related to handling sensitive data.

Implementing these recommendations will reduce risks to assets, enhance data confidentiality, and improve overall adherence to security and compliance best practices.
